About Swaraj Builders

Building dreams into reality with over 25 years of excellence in construction and development.

OUR STORY

Founded in 1998 by Rajesh Swaraj, Swaraj Builders began as a small construction company with a vision to transform the real estate landscape of Pune. What started with a single residential project has grown into one of Pune's most trusted construction companies. Over the years, we have successfully completed 50+ residential and commercial projects, housing thousands of families and numerous businesses. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition and trust in the industry. Today, Swaraj Builders stands as a symbol of reliability and excellence, continuing to build not just structures, but communities and lasting relationships.
